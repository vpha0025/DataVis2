Victoria Road Death 2019

The fatal accident data is a dataset which outlines all road death within Victoria.
It includes records such as accident number, date, age and gender (just to name a few) between the years
2005 and 2020.
The aim of this visualisation, is to highlight the interesting features surrounding Road death in Victoria in 2019

There are many different factors that cause fatal road
accidents. One of the important factors is age. Depending
on the age group there are different components such as
health or even social influence, just to name a few.

Due to human nature there will always be stereotypes
between man and woman. In this case, the stereotype is
that men are more reckless on the street than women
This visualisation suggest that those perceptions are true.
